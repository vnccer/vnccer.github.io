    Columns#

    Columns help organize shorter pieces of content horizontally for readability. columns shortcode styles markdown list as up to 3 columns.

    Example#

     1{{% columns [ratio="1:1"] [class="..."] %}}
 2- ### Left Content
 3  Lorem markdownum insigne...
 4
 5- ### Mid Content
 6  Lorem markdownum insigne...
 7
 8- ### Right Content
 9  Lorem markdownum insigne...
10{{% /columns %}}
